You gotta listen to this!

Lucy Dacus

I'm a big Lucy Dacus fan. Last summer I saw her play a show on a farm in Washington, which ended up being one of the best concerts I've been to. No Burden is probably my favorite album of hers, even though it is one of her saddest.

Big Thief

Big Thief is one of my favorite bands. They balance playful songs like Spud Infinity with more reflective tracks like Change, sometimes on the same album. Their new album, Double Infinity, quickly became my favorite, and honestly I don't think they have any misses in their discography.

Aliceband

Aliceband is a really interesting group. The lead singer, Alice Walker (not that Alice Walker), mixes jazzy rhythms and instrumentation with a more pop-leaning sound. Their self-titled album is probably the best place to start, but I also like some others like Red River.

Fleet Foxes

Fleet Foxes is my go-to background music. I like putting one of their albums on while reading or working. There are some upbeat songs like Mykonos, but most of their music has a calm, atmospheric feel.
