Lucy Dacus

I'm a big Lucy Dacus fan. Last summer I saw her play a show on a farm in Washington, which ended up being one of the best concerts I've been to. No Burden is probably my favorite album of hers, even though it is one of her saddest.

Big Thief

Big Thief is one of my favorite bands. Their new album, Double Infinity, has quickly become my favorite, and I don't think they have any bad songs in their entire discography.

Aliceband

Aliceband is a really interesting group. The lead singer, Alice Walker (not that Alice Walker), mixes jazzy rhythms and instruments with a more pop-leaning sound..

Fleet Foxes

Fleet Foxes is my go-to background music. I like putting one of their albums on while reading or working. There are some upbeat songs like Mykonos, but most of their music has a calm, atmospheric feel.
